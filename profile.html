Company Profile
Unidot, established in 1997, in Chennai, India, is a pioneer in the business of processing photopolymer plates. Our years of experience produce the finest, crispest, most detailed polymer plates. Consistent quality. Competitive prices. Impeccable customer service. Experienced human plate makers with a perfectionist streaks. What more do you want from your platemaking service? A quick turnaround? Wait a second - we have a quick turnaround! We make deliveries on the same day!.
   
Unidot has in-house facilities for colour separations and graphic manipulation, and is a complete pre-press house providing services to Flexo printers. It has also been engaged in the production and processing of pre-press products required for all types of printing like, photopolymer plates from the thinnest for computer stationery and label printers to the thicker grades for plastic bags, and the thickest for corrugated board printing.
We provide photopolymer plates for flexograpgic printing, flexographic plate making equipment, analog colour proofing systems and digital proofing solutions. We are the stockists for Dupont and other graphic art products.
